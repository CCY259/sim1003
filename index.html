Week 6

Show that

$$\int_{-\pi}^\pi \sin^{2026} x \,dx = \frac{\pi}{2^{2025}}\binom{2026}{1013}.$$

Hint: De Moivre's Theorem (Please do not expand using $\sin^2x = \frac{1-\cos 2x}{2}$!)
